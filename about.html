A sound story

One of my first musical memories are the bicycle rides with my dad. We used to whistle melodies along the way. Cannot exactly remember my age but was small enough to fit in the bike’s basket.
On my adolescence I have enrolled on a local music school to learn classical guitar. At that moment my passion for music was overwhelming and I found my calling.
Circumstances made me move to Wales, UK, where I had vocal lessons on the former Yale College. Around this time my husband, also a musician, influenced me to compose and record my first original work as Deep Dream. It was a “garage band” where I gained some insight about composing and recording.
Returning to Portugal I decided to take my musical ventures to the stage and to the classroom. I, then started working as a vocal teacher on a music school (RockGP) and joined several cover bands. Over the years I had the opportunity to perform in pubs, hotels, casinos, private parties, to name a few.
